Our Story

Founded in 2025, TechNova emerged from a simple vision: to make quality IT education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location.

What started as a small training center with just 10 students has grown into a comprehensive learning platform serving over 1000 students worldwide.

Our curriculum is constantly updated to reflect the latest industry trends and technologies, ensuring our students are always learning the most relevant and in-demand skills.
